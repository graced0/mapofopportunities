About Us

(Meghana G., Victoria C., and Grace D.)

We were inspired by our desire to be able to easily find and discover new experiences without having to rely on our parents
and teachers all the time. Our program was also created because we thought it was important to support the building of relationships
with new people since we all met at the HackTJ event, 24 hours before we finished our project together.
